Ingredient

  • Meat Sauce: 1 lb sweet Italian sausage, ¾ lb lean ground beef, ½ cup minced onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes, 2 (6.5 oz) cans tomato sauce, 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste, and ½ cup water
  • Seasoning: 2 tbsp white sugar, 1.5 tsp dried basil, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 0.5 tsp fennel seeds, salt, and pepper.
  • Cheese Layer: 16 oz ricotta cheese, 1 large egg, ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese, and ¾ lb mozzarella cheese (sliced or shredded).
  • Pasta: 12 lasagna noodles.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Make the Meat Sauce: In a large pot, brown the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat. Stir in the tomatoes, sauce, paste, water, and seasonings. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally
  2. Boil the Noodles: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the 12 lasagna noodles for 8–10 minutes until al dente, drain, and rinse with cold water.
  3. Mix the Cheese: In a bowl, thoroughly combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and 2 tbsp parsley.
  4. Layer the Lasagna: Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise, top with half the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella, and another 1.5 cups of meat sauce. Repeat with another layer of noodles, the remaining ricotta, a third of the mozzarella, and more sauce. Finish with a layer of pasta, remaining sauce, and the last of the mozzarella and Parmesan
  5. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil (spray it with cooking spray so the cheese doesn't stick) and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly
  6. Rest: Let the lasagna sit at room temperature for 15–20 minutes before slicing so the layers can set.
